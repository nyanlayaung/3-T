History

The University of London was established by Royal Charter in 1836 for the public benefit and is recognised globally. Throughout our long hisotry, the University has offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities.

Archery

At the University of London Archery Club, we practice archery both indoors and outdoors. We aim to accommodate everyone, whether you are looking to compete or just have fun.

Dancesport

Students studying at any of the University of London university or college are welcome to join the University of London(UoL) Dancesport team and represent London in Ballroom.

Ice Hockey Dragons

The London Dragons were originally founded in 2002 by members of the Imperial College Hockey Club as the Purples.
