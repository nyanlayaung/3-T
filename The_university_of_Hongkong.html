Hong kong university of science and technology

History

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (abbreviated as HKUST) is an English-language public research university in Clear Water Bay Peninsula, New Territories, Hong Kong. Founded in 1991 by the British Hong Kong Government, it was the territory's third institution to be granted university status.

Activities

Dragon boat racing

A dragon boat is a human-powered watercraft originating from the Pearl River Delta region of China's southern Guangdong Province. These were made of teak, but in other parts of China, different kinds of wood are used.
